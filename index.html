Programs & Curriculum

Four-stage pathway from Explorer to Innovator with practical capstones.
Hands-on • Project-led • Edge AI

Stage 1 — Explorer (Ages 8–12)

Introduction to technology, safe creativity, basic electronics, visual coding.

  • Paper circuits, LED lamps, solar toy fan
  • Digital citizenship & lab safety

Stage 2 — Builders Foundation (Ages 12–16)

Circuit design, Arduino, programming in C, and embedded projects.

  • Traffic lights, temperature monitors, obstacle robots

Stage 3 — Builders (Ages 15–20)

Embedded systems, IoT, basic ML, Edge AI demos on microcontrollers.

Stage 4 — Innovator (Ages 18–25)

Advanced robotics, sensor fusion, deployment to Jetson/Coral, entrepreneurship & IP.

Safety & Child Protection

Our Safety & Consent plan ensures a secure, respectful, supervised learning space.
Safeguards • Consent • First-aid

Key elements

  • Background checks & child-safety orientation for staff
  • Teacher-to-student ratio 1:10 (max); no staff alone with minors
  • Mandatory safety induction before practicals
  • Equipment inspection, safe storage, first-aid & fire extinguisher
  • Digital safety, parental consent for data/photos
  • Incident logbook and immediate parent notification

Safety Plan (Short)

Purpose: Secure, respectful, supervised learning.
Child protection: Background checks; 1:10 ratio; attendance logged.
Lab safety: Inductions, PPE, inspections, hazardous materials supervised.
Digital safety: Privacy, parental consent, monitored platforms.
Emergency: Visible exits, first-aid responder, incident log.
Ethics: Inclusive, report concerns confidentially.
Review: Annual; aligned with UNICEF & British Council guidelines.
