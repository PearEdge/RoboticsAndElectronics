Hands-On Learning in Robotics, Electronics & Edge AI

At PearEdge Robotics & Electronics Club, we’re empowering Africa’s next generation of innovators through hands-on learning in robotics, electronics, and Edge AI.

From assembling their first circuits to building intelligent systems that work offline, students don’t just learn technology - they create it.

Our programs are designed for kids and teens aged 8–25, guiding them from curiosity to creativity, and from basic circuits to real-world AI applications that solve African problems.

We believe Africa’s future engineers are already here - they just need the right tools, mentors, and opportunities to shine.

Programs & Curriculum

Four-stage pathway from Explorer to Innovator with practical capstones.
Hands-on • Project-led

Stage 1: Explorer (Ages 8–12)

Introduction to technology, safe creativity, basic electronics, visual coding.

Stage 2: Builders Foundation (Ages 12–16)

Circuit design, Arduino, programming in C, and embedded projects.

Stage 3: Builders (Ages 15–20)

Embedded systems, IoT, basic ML, Edge AI demos on microcontrollers.

Stage 4 — Innovator (Ages 18–25)

Advanced robotics, sensor fusion, deployment to Jetson/Coral, entrepreneurship & IP.
